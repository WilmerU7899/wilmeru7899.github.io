SHABR Website Project

About the Project:

The purpose of this repository is to help you practice using Bootstrap pieces: grid, content, components, utilities, etc.

Context:

My college roommate is not only a hot air balloon enthusiast, but he just got his pilot's license and is ready to start a business!

Of course, my roommate needs a website built, so obviously, they turn to me! My roommate has agreed to give me 10% of the profits during the first 2 years of business! It is in my best interest to make this website look as good as possible!

My roommate has given me the content, so all I have to do is put my Bootstrap skills to the test!
